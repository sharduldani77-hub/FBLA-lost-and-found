Our Mission


Our mission here at Tompkins Trove is simple. To provide you and the Tompkins community with easy access to register and claim lost items. Ever since V1, that has been our intention. Through V2, we give you a more focused registration process, a refined search engine, and a more secure claim process. These are just many more of the improvements that come with V2 of Tompkins Trove.
